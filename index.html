About the Author

SANDRA EKPENYONG

Forensic Biblical Storyteller • Publishing Strategist • Cultural Thought Leader

Sandra Ekpenyong is a forensic biblical storyteller, publishing strategist, and cultural thought leader whose work explores the intersection of faith, leadership, and human behavior. She is the founder of World Harvest International Ministries and Joseph Dream Publishing, where she develops transformational content and frameworks centered on identity, power, and emotional restoration, while helping authors build books and scalable intellectual property ecosystems. Sandra has contributed to global conversations on women, crisis, and resilience. She served as a panelist with Voice of America on the Chibok Girls abduction, participating alongside human rights advocates, policymakers, and global voices engaged in addressing the crisis and its aftermath. With a background in Political Science from the University of Maryland and over two decades of leadership experience, Sandra's work uncovers hidden emotional and behavioral patterns within individuals and systems. Through her writing, speaking, and publishing platforms, she equips individuals to confront silence, reclaim identity, and lead with clarity, integrity, and purpose.
